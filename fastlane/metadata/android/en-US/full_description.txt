Keep your screen awake with Coffee, this simple utility keeps your screen awake right when you need it.

Most devices auto-dim screens to save battery during reading, navigation, or recipes. However, this can be frustrating when you need the display on. Coffee lets you lock the screen awake easily.

<b>Features:</b>

- One-tap Quick Settings tile toggle.
- Force screen awake with custom timers or indefinite.
- Lightweight with no ads.

<b>Important Note:</b>

Screen awake mode uses foreground service and WAKE_LOCK, which may impact battery. Toggle off when done.
